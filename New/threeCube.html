3x3x3 Rubik's Cube

The 3x3x3 Rubik's Cube is the classic puzzle that has been puzzling people since the 80s.

Method

My method is the CFOP method, also known as the Fridrich method. Invented by Jessica Fridrich, the method is extremely popular, used by most of the world's fastest speedsolvers. The method is done in 4 steps:

  1. Cross - Solve four edges of the same color
  2. First Two Layers (F2L) - Solve corners and edges in between the solved cross pieces to solve the first two layers of the cube.
  3. Orient (the) Last Layer (OLL) - Orient all of the pieces in the last layer, making the top all one color
  4. Permute (the) Last Layer (PLL) - Solve corners and edges in between the solved cross pieces.
