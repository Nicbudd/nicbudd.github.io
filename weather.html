Weather Links

As a weather enthusiast, I've always found it difficult to find resources on the internet to find meteorological data, so I've compiled a set of links that I believe will be helpful resources for weather enthusiasts such as myself. There are a few charts for different categories ranked roughly by how usefel I find them.

Tropical Weather

https://www.nhc.noaa.govNational Hurricane Center (NWS)

Official National Hurricane Center guidance on tropical systems. 2 Day & 5 Day tropical weather outlooks and impact guidance.

https://www.tropicaltidbits.comDr. Levi Cowan

Collection of commonly used tools. Blog posts with a professional opinion on US-affecting tropical cyclones, current storm information, satellite imagery, forecast models, aircraft reconnaissance data, climate analysis tools.

http://weather.cod.edu/satrad/exper/College of Dupage

GOES-16 & GOES-17 easy to navigate high quality visualization with localized imagery sectors. Overlays for extra data visualization.

https://cyclonicwx.com/recon/Alex Boreham

Recon data

Detailed Sea Surface Temperature analysis images.

https://rammb-data.cira.colostate.edu/tc_realtime/CIRA (CSU)

Compilation and archive of storm imagery and data. Many graphs of numerical product data, mainly regarding TC intensity forecasts.

https://bmcnoldy.earth.miami.edu/Brian McNoldy
